## âš¡ Quick Sourcing Tips

### Best Sources (In Order):
1. **Wikimedia Commons** (commons.wikimedia.org)
   - Largest collection
   - Easy download
   - Clear licensing

2. **Library of Congress** (loc.gov)
   - Excellent for American history
   - All public domain
   - High quality scans

3. **National Archives** (archives.gov)
   - Government documents
   - Executive orders
   - Congressional photos

4. **Internet Archive** (archive.org)
   - Historical books
   - Old newspapers
   - Public domain images

### Search Strategy:
1. Add "public domain" to any search
2. Add date range (e.g., "1910-1920")
3. Try both modern and historical spellings
4. Look for museum archives

### Download Tips:
- Get **highest resolution available** (at least 1200px wide)
- **Save as JPG** for photographs
- **Name exactly as shown** in IMAGE_GUIDE.md
- Place in correct `/public/images/[chapter]/` folder
